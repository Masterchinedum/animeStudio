# Novel Canon Ledger

## Chapter 001

Ren Sato is summoned by Chairman Kang to audit the death of Director Han. Ren is granted temporary Tier-5 access to investigate the Ledger, under the strict surveillance of Captain Marcus Vance, and discovers that Han's collateral was stolen using code containing his own secret Erasure Protocol signature.

### Facts
- Director Han was a Tier-6 executive who was demoted to Tier 2 and died in an apparent suicide.
- Chairman Kang Dae-Jung is a Tier-8 regional syndicate boss.
- Captain Marcus Vance is a Tier-5 head of private security for Kang Global.
- Ren Sato is given temporary Tier-5 administrative auditor access for seventy-two hours, mirrored directly to Marcus Vance's terminal.
- Director Han's collateral consisted of unredacted manifests of Sector 4 pharmaceutical distribution networks (narcotics).
- The collateral was not leaked but moved to a ghost directory inside the Kang Global local node.
- Ren finds his own unique Erasure Protocol compiler signature within the unauthorized system bypass code of Han's account.

### Open threads
- Who obtained Ren's secret Erasure Protocol code to execute the bypass?
- Where is Han's stolen collateral currently being held within the tower?
- How will Ren complete the audit without Marcus Vance discovering the true nature of the code?

## Chapter 002

Ren Sato attends a high-society charity gala hosted by Mei-Ling Vance to observe Director Han's former associates. Mei-Ling corners Ren in her private showroom, showing intimate knowledge of his personal routines and grey-market purchases, establishing leverage before slipping him a micro-drive containing evidence that Han's death was an execution ordered from within Kang's inner circle.

### Facts
- Ren Sato attended the Vance Haute Couture charity gala.
- Mei-Ling Vance knows about Ren's weekly trips to the Slag District and his purchase of grey-market pulmonary inhalers using diverted corporate funds.
- Mei-Ling possesses and gave Ren a physical micro-drive containing Director Han's raw communications from the forty-eight hours leading up to his demotion.
- Chairman Kang was absent from the gala, ostensibly managing a restructuring committee.
- Ren's suit is an altered off-the-rack garment, reflecting his Tier 4 status.
- Mei-Ling's private showroom has military-grade acoustic dampening and white-noise generators.

### Open threads
- How did Director Han obtain code from Ren's secret Erasure Protocol?
- Who within Chairman Kang's inner circle ordered Director Han's execution?
- What specific data is on the micro-drive Mei-Ling gave to Ren?

## Chapter 003

Ren and Yuki decrypt Mei-Ling's micro-drive in Ren's secure apartment. The drive reveals a target list of Kang Syndicate executives linked to a blackmailer known as 'The Tailor.' The decryption triggers a silent network sweep by Marcus Vance's security forces, forcing Ren to physically destroy his secondary server and flee.

### Facts
- Mei-Ling's drive was decrypted using Ren's secondary, air-gapped server.
- The drive contained a target list including Director Han (deceased), Vice-President Choi, Director Sterling, and Director Vance.
- The blackmailer's cryptographic signature is 'The Tailor.'
- Vice-President Choi's collateral is scheduled to leak in forty-eight hours.
- The micro-drive contained a passive transponder that alerted Marcus Vance's security division to its location upon decryption.
- Ren physically destroyed his secondary server with a ceramic spike and set fire to the chassis to prevent data retrieval.
- Yuki escaped via the service elevator, while Ren fled into the night.

### Open threads
- How will Marcus Vance react to the destroyed server and the brief transponder signal?
- Where will Ren and Yuki hide now that Ren's apartment is compromised?
- How will Ren use his forty-eight-hour predictive advantage regarding Vice-President Choi?

## Chapter 004

Ren visits his erased brother Kenji in the Slag District to deliver medical supplies and warn him of danger. After learning that corporate sweepers are searching for Kenji, Ren spends half his personal savings to relocate him to a safehouse in Sector 9. Kenji reveals a salvaged corporate comm-device that is receiving encrypted pings from a Tier-8 transmitter located inside the Slag District.

### Facts
- Ren's brother Kenji is a Tier-0 Erased (Muen) living in the Slag District as a mechanic.
- Ren delivers six months of corporate-grade bronchodilators and antibiotics to Kenji.
- Unmarked corporate sweepers with thermal scanners are actively searching the Slag District for Kenji.
- Ren spends eighty thousand credits in physical bearer chips (half his escape fund) to relocate Kenji to a safehouse in Sector 9 run by Chen.
- Kenji salvaged a cracked Ledger-linked comm-unit from an Apex scrap heap.
- The salvaged comm-unit is receiving encrypted pings from a Tier-8 transmitter broadcasting from within the Slag District.

### Open threads
- Who is operating the Tier-8 transmitter in the Slag District?
- Are the sweepers working for Kang, Apex, or another entity?
- Will Kenji safely reach the Sector 9 safehouse?

## Chapter 005

Ren Sato witnesses the sudden demotion of Vice-President Choi from Tier 5 to Tier 1 during an audit interview, after Choi's collateral is leaked. Captain Marcus Vance arrests Choi, who screams about 'the tailor' holding his wife before being dragged away.

### Facts
- Vice-President Choi was a Tier-5 executive in charge of Maritime Logistics at Kang Global.
- Choi's collateral involved a signed confession regarding union strikes at Pier 12.
- The leak of Choi's collateral dropped his rating to Tier 1 instantly, freezing his assets and terminating his housing.
- The blackmailer timed the leak precisely during Ren's interview with Choi.
- Marcus Vance and Kang Private Security arrested Choi immediately following his demotion.
- Choi claimed 'the tailor' took his wife and had the encryption keys.
- The smart-glass walls in high-tier offices frost over automatically when an occupant's rating drops below Tier 3.

### Open threads
- Who is 'the tailor' Choi referred to?
- How did the blackmailer obtain Choi's biometric or cryptographic keys to leak his collateral?
- Will Ren deliver the audit report to Marcus Vance by morning as demanded?
